About Our Clinic

100SMILES Dental Care is dedicated to providing high-quality dental services for families, children, adults and seniors. Located at 33 Fantail Street, Razole, our clinic is equipped with modern dental technology and a compassionate team committed to making every patient feel safe and welcome.

With over 50 years of combined clinical experience, our dental team offers a full range of services including general dentistry, orthodontics, emergency care, cosmetic treatments, implants and preventative health checks. We pride ourselves on delivering care that is gentle, reliable and tailored to every individual.
